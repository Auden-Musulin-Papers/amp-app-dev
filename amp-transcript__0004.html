0017                    Dear Stella:
0018        Many thanks for your nice long letter. Stephen has a genius for
0019     subtle misrepresentation. "Serious insistence on unseriousness'
0020     telescopes two cnvictuonsdistinct convictions of mine,falsifying both.
0021      I) I believe it to be a serious moral error when an artist overestimates
0022         the importance of art and,by implication,of himself. One must admit
0023         that the plotical political history of Euriope,with all itsthe same horrors,
0024         would be what it has been,if Dante,Shakespeare,Goethe,Titian,
0025         Moxzart,et al,had never existed.
0026     2) I believe that the only way in which,to-day at any rate,one can
0027        speak seroiu s eriously seriously about serious matters(the alternaticve
0028        is silence) is comically. I am surprised to hear from you that
0029        Jews find this hard to accept. I have enormously admired - and been
0030       influenced by - the tradition of Jewish humor. More than any other
0031       people,surely,they have seen in serious matters,that is to say,human
0032       suffering,and the contradictions of human existence,and the relation
0033       between man and God,occasions for humorous expression.
0034        e.g,: "If the rich could hire other people to die for them,the poor
0035      couold make a wonderful living", or "Truth rests with God alone,and
0036      a little bit with me",or "God will provide - ah,if only He wouold till
0037      He does so."
0038   So! you encountered the one-whose-name-we-never-mention. Why Chestrer should
0039  have been so foolish as  to invite him to a pearty,I cannot imagine. If he
0040 is to be seen at all,he jymust be seen alone. Incidentally,next time you see
0041 Chester,scold him a) for not writing to me b) for not answering Harrison's
0042 cable.
0043  All well here.except for a leaking kitvchen roof. No doubt you read about
0044 our and .(My typewriter is on the blink,hence the
0045 hiatuses.)  A firen friend of mine who teaches schyzophrenics,has a seventeen
0046 year old girl who is interested in poetry. Asked what poets she liked,she
0047 mentioned me. "iI happen to know him quite well",said my friend. To which the
0048 girl in astonishment:"You mean to say,he's still alive!".
0049   Expect to get back to Kirchstetten about April 20th.
0050                      much love and greetings to son and mum-in-law
0051 Wystan.
