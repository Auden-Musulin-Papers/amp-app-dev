0016 Nov 8th


0017 Dear Stella.
0018 A disaster has happened. Frau Eiermann
0019 died of a heart-attack on Nov 4th.
0020 (Hav just heard this from my lawyer.).
0021 A minor worry connected with this is what
0022 is happening to her cats and ours1/2. I suppose
0023 they will probably have to be destroyed.
0024 Next time you are travelling between Fridau
0025 and Wien , could you be a dear and stop
0026 in Kirchstetten to see what is happening.
0027 Incedentally, in case you should need it my lawyers
0028 name and address is Dr Walter Redlich. Wien I.
0029 Wipplingerstrasse, 24-26.
0030 was quite fun. The Archbishop came
0031 to my first lecture.
0032 love
0033 Wystan
