0014      JOSEPH WEINHEBER
0015        (I892-I945)


(vl) 0001Reachijng my gate a narrow
(vl) 0002Lane from the village
(vl) 0003Passes on into a wood:
(vl) 0004When I walk that way
(vl) 0005It seems becoming to stop
(vl) 0006And look through the fence
(vl) 0007Of your garden where(under
(vl) 0008The circs they had to)
(vl) 0009They buried you like a loved
(vl) 0010Old family dog.


(vl) 0001Categorised enemies
(vl) 0002Twenty years ago,
(vl) 0003Now next-door neighbors,we might
(vl) 0004Have become good friends,
(vl) 0005Sharing a common ambit
(vl) 0006And love of the word,
(vl) 0007Over a golden Kremser
(vl) 0008Had many a long
(vl) 0009Language on syntax,commas,
(vl) 0010Versification.


(vl) 0001Yes,yes,it has to be said:
(vl) 0002Men of great damage
(vl) 0003And malengine took you up.
(vl) 0004Did they,though,ever
(vl) 0005Take you in,who to Goebbels'
(vl) 0006Offer of culture
(vl) 0007Countered In Ruah lassen!?
(vl) 0008But the crowd prefers
(vl) 0009A nasty stink and the young
(vl) 0010Condemn you unread.


(vl) 0001What,had you ever heard of
(vl) 0002Franz Jägerstätter,
(vl) 0003The St.Radegund peasant
(vl) 0004Who said his lonely
(vl) 0005Nein to the Aryan State
(vl) 0006And was beheaded,
(vl) 0007Would your heart,as Austrian,
(vl) 0008As poet,have told you?
(vl) 0009Good care,of course,was taken
(vl) 0010You should hear nothing,


(vl) 0001Be unprepared for a day
(vl) 0002That was bound to come,
(vl) 0003A season of dread and tears
(vl) 0004And dishevelment
(vl) 0005When,transfixed by a nightmare,
(vl) 0006You destroyed yourself.
(vl) 0007Retribution was ever
(vl) 0008A bungler at it:
(vl) 0009Dies alles ist furchtbar,hier
(vl) 0010Nur Schweigen gemäss.(I)


0016 (2


(vl) 0001Unmarked by me,unmourned for,
(vl) 0002The hour of your death,
(vl) 0003Unhailed by you the moment
(vl) 0004When,providence-led,
(vl) 0005I first beheld Kirchstetten
(vl) 0006On a pouring wet
(vl) 0007October day in a year
(vl) 0008That changed our Cosmos,
(vl) 0009The annus mirabilis
(vl) 0010When Parity fell.


(vl) 0001Already the realms that lost
(vl) 0002Were properly warm
(vl) 0003And over-eating,their crimes
(vl) 0004The pedestrian
(vl) 0005Private sort,those nuisances,
(vl) 0006Corpses and rubble,
(vl) 0007Lonfg carted away: for their raped
(vl) 0008The shock was fading,
(vl) 0009Their kidnapped physicists felt
(vl) 0010No longer homesick.


(vl) 0001To-day we smile at weddings
(vl) 0002Where bride and bridegroom
(vl) 0003Were both born since the Shadow
(vl) 0004Lifted,or rather
(vl) 0005Moved elsewhere. Never as yet
(vl) 0006Has Earth been without
(vl) 0007Her bad patch,some unplace with
(vl) 0008Jobs for torturers
(vl) 0009(In what bars are they welcome?
(vl) 0010What girls marry them?),


(vl) 0001Or her nutritive surface
(vl) 0002At peace all over.
(vl) 0003No one,so far as we know,
(vl) 0004Has ever been safe,
(vl) 0005And so in secret regions
(vl) 0006Good family men
(vl) 0007Keep eye,devoted as monks,
(vl) 0008On apparatus
(vl) 0009Inside which harmless matter
(vl) 0010Turns homicidal.


(vl) 0001Here,though,I feel as at home
(vl) 0002As you did: the same
(vl) 0003Short-lived creatures re-utter
(vl) 0004The same care-free songs,
(vl) 0005Orchards cling to the regime
(vl) 0006They know,from April's
(vl) 0007Rapid augment of color
(vl) 0008Till boisterous Fall
(vl) 0009When at each stammering gust
(vl) 0010Apples thump the ground.

(vl) 0002Neighbor and colleague,

(vl) 0005The workmanship and the note

0022          Dear Stella:
0023  Kirchstetten is intending to have a ceroemony in memory of the twentieth
0024  anniversary of Weinheber'ws  death.  Herewith my effort to do my
