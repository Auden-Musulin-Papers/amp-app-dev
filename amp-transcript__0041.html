      0022 Dear Stella:
      0023 Have just finished reading Austria"
      0024 by which I was enchanted as well as
      0025 instructed. My! how erudite you are, Where did
      0026 you dig up all those stories ?
      0027 A trifle alarmed that it may attract more
      0028 tourists,which I'm sure you don't welcome
      0029 any more than I do. (Thanks , by the way,
      0030 for the reference to my garage.).
      0031 Am very curious to learn more about "The Life
      0032 and Adventure s of Peter Prosch."       Is it in print?
      0033 Shouldn't it be translated?
      0034 As you say, the relation betwen art and society
      0035 is very odd. Why should the Vienna of 1890-1918
      0036 have produced such a florescence,? (Chester can
      0037 appreciate Bruckner better than I).. Even odder to
      0038 me is the Biedermayer period, No one can
      0039 possibly approve of Metternich's police state,yet,
      0040 in a more liberal mileieu, could Stifter,
      0041 Raimund, Nestroy, have written what they did?
      0042 Hope this reaches you. Looking forward to seeing you
      0043 in Sept[]
      0044 love and admiration Wystan
